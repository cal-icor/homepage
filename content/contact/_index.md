## What to Include

To help us respond effectively, consider sharing:

- Your institution (UC, CSU, or CCC campus)
- Your role (instructor, administrator, IT staff)
- Courses or programs you're interested in
- Your timeline for getting started

Don't worry if you don't have all the details yet. We're happy to explore options with you.
