A Statewide Infrastructure for Equitable Access to Industry-Standard Programming and Data Science Tools

Cal-ICOR (California Interactive Computing Open Resource) is a UC Berkeley-led initiative supported by the California Education Learning Lab. Our mission is to bring high-quality, cloud-hosted Jupyter environments and modular data-science curricula to public colleges and universities across California with zero local installation, zero software maintenance, and equal access for every student, regardless of device or financial and educational background.

Our mission is simple: ensure that every student in California has access to the tools, skills, and learning environments that prepare them for data-rich careers and civic participation.

Why Partner with Cal-ICOR

Cal-ICOR makes it simple for California educators to bring modern computing into their classrooms without technical barriers or extra workload. By joining the network, institutions gain access to a shared cloud-based JupyterHub, hands-on support, and teaching environments designed for real data science learning. We partner directly with faculty and campus teams so they can focus on instruction, not infrastructure.
